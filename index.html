Money Projections

This is Projections

This project is a project to make a calculator which will be able to find out just how much extra you have based on how much you have in your account(s), cost of bills, extra spending, and duration of time.

What it's Not

This calculator will not factor investments, it will not store any amounts, nor will it be intended to legally provide financial advice and all figures should be double-checked.
